Engineering
Services
HBT Engineering provides comprehensive engineering services supporting the design, development, and validation of complex industrial systems. Our multidisciplinary teams combine expertise across mechanical engineering, hydraulics, gearbox design, electrical systems, system integration, and virtual validation to deliver robust engineering solutions. These services support customers across the entire product lifecycle — from concept design to production readiness. The company’s global engineering expertise is built on nearly 200 years of industrial engineering heritage originating from Luenen, Germany.
