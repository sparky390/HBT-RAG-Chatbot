Automation and
Software Solutions
HBT Engineering provides end-to-end
automation and industrial software development services
for OEMs, industrial equipment manufacturers, and digital manufacturing platforms.
Our teams support the complete technology stack — from
embedded firmware and control systems to industrial software platforms, testing automation, and hardware integration
. We help organizations accelerate product development, modernize industrial systems, and deploy reliable automation solutions at scale.
